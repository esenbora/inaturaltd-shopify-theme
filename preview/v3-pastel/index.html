Our story

A grove in the Aegean.
A flat in Tottenham.
One small line of skincare.

Seyda and Ferhat grew up between Aegean olive groves and London winters. They moved to the UK with two suitcases and a quietly stubborn idea: bring the rituals they trusted at home to the people they now lived next to.

INature is what came out of it — eight formulas, made by women in Türkiye, shipped from a tiny warehouse in N17. No PR firm, no celebrity launch, no influencer codes. Just a bottle, a wax-sealed envelope, and a thank-you note.

— Seyda & Ferhat Founders · Tottenham, London
