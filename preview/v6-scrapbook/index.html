a note from the founders

"We brought a recipe,
two suitcases,
and a stubborn idea."

Seyda and Ferhat grew up between Aegean olive groves and London winters. They moved to the UK with two suitcases and a quietly stubborn idea: bring the rituals they trusted at home to the people they now lived next to.

INature is what came out of it — eight formulas, made by women in a halal-certified facility in Türkiye, packed into a small warehouse in N17. Every parcel leaves wax-sealed, with a handwritten thank-you.

— Seyda & Ferhat Founders · N17, London
