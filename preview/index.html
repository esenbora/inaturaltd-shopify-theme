Our story

Trusted in Europe for 5 years.
Now ready for your family in the UK.

Seyda and Ferhat brought their trust in INCIA's natural and eco-friendly products from Türkiye to the UK. They realised how difficult it was for friends with newborns to find truly natural, safe products in Britain.

INCIA is already trusted across Europe through HemNature — including retail partnerships with ETOS in the Netherlands. INature was created to make these same clean, gentle, 100% natural products accessible to families across the UK.
