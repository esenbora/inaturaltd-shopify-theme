Frequently asked questions.

🌿 Brand & Origins

Who is INCIA and what does INature do?
INCIA is a natural skincare brand established in Türkiye, trusted across Europe via HemNature (NL, BE) including partnerships with ETOS. INature is the official UK distributor — we import, stock and ship INCIA's full natural range from our London warehouse.
What's the meaning behind the name INature?
"I" + "Nature" — a personal commitment to natural ingredients. The capital I is a deliberate stylistic choice to reflect the brand's clean, confident identity.
When was INature founded?
INature UK Ltd was founded in 2024 by Seyda and Ferhat. The parent brand INCIA has been operating for over 5 years across Europe.

🧴 Product Quality & Ingredients

Are INature products 100% natural?
Yes — every formula is 100% natural origin. We never use synthetic fragrances, parabens, SLS, fluoride, mineral oils or phenoxyethanol. All ingredients are listed transparently on each product page.
What certifications do you hold?
Every INCIA formula carries five independent certifications: ETKO Cosmos (natural & organic cosmetics), BioArge (organic R&D), Vegan, Cruelty-Free, and Baby-Friendly.
Are products safe during pregnancy and for babies?
Most of our range is pregnancy and breastfeeding-safe. Baby-specific products (Baby Shampoo, Baby Oil, Stretch Mark Cream, Nipple Cream) are dermatologically tested for newborn skin. Always check individual product pages for full ingredient lists.

🌍 Ethics & Environment

Do you test on animals?
Never. Zero animal testing — not in research, not in production, not ever. All formulas are tested only on human volunteers.
Is the packaging recyclable?
Yes — glass bottles, aluminium caps and FSC-certified paper across the range. We're working towards refillable formats for 2026.
