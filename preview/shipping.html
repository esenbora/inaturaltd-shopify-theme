↩️ Returns & Exchanges

We do everything to ensure you don't need to return your products. Before ordering, consider:

  • Seek advice — if you're unsure about a product, contact us at info@inatureltd.com or DM @inatureuk on Instagram.
  • Read the product page — full ingredient lists, INCI codes and usage instructions are listed on every product page.

If you still need to return an item:

  1. You have 14 days from receipt to return the order.
  2. Return shipping costs are at your own expense (unless the item is faulty).
  3. Products must be returned in their original condition and packaging, unused and undamaged, with all accessories.
  4. Email us first at info@inatureltd.com with your order number to start a return.

Damage from handling: Some products are delicate. If a product is damaged due to dropping or bumping during your use, we unfortunately cannot assist. Please handle and store products with care.
