◐ A note from the founders

A small boutique,
started in N17 with
two suitcases and a
stubborn idea.

Seyda and Ferhat grew up between Aegean olive groves and London winters. They moved to the UK with two suitcases and a quietly stubborn idea: bring the rituals they trusted at home to the people they now lived next to.

INature is what came out of it — eight formulas, made by women in a halal-certified Turkish atelier, dispatched from a small London warehouse. No PR firm, no celebrity launch, no influencer codes. Just the bottle, and a note.
