Our story

Trusted in Europe for 5 years.
Now ready for your family in the UK.

INature is the official UK distributor of INCIA Naturals — the natural skincare brand trusted by 50,000+ families across the Netherlands, Belgium and beyond.

INature was founded by Seyda and Ferhat in 2024, two friends who brought their trust in INCIA's natural and eco-friendly products from Türkiye to the UK.

They soon realised how difficult it was for their close friends — especially those with newborns and young children — to find truly natural, safe products in Britain. Reading the back of every bottle. Wading through long ingredient lists. Wondering which brands actually meant it.

INCIA is a well-known and trusted brand in Türkiye and worldwide, distributed across Europe through HemNature — with partnerships including retailers such as ETOS in the Netherlands. So the trust was already there. The supply chain was already there. We just needed to bring it home.

INature was created to make these same clean, gentle, 100% natural products accessible to families across the UK.

Every formula we stock is short, dermatologically tested, and certified — ETKO Cosmos, BioArge, Vegan and Cruelty-Free. The same shelf in ETOS, now on your shelf in N17.

— Seyda & Ferhat, founders
Tottenham, London · 2024
